The Story of Us

Discover the journey of how two hearts found each other and fell in love as we share our unique love story with you.

"Two Souls, One Journey: The Enchanting Tale of Abrar and Aljosa's Wedding"

  • On How We Met

    Abrar: Let us weave for you the tale of our fateful encounter. Picture it – the gym, an unlikely place for destiny to unfold. There was Aljosa, engaged in a rather comical exercise, the "Duck Walk," squatting down like he was tiptoeing to a secret.
    Aljosa: Abrar, meanwhile, was hard at work training a client. My antics caught her eye, and her laughter filled the air like a sweet melody. It was in that laughter that our story truly began.
    Abrar: From that day forward, Aljosa couldn't help but want to know me better. He set out to impress me with his unique sense of humor, and he dreamt of courting me with all the love in his heart.
    Aljosa: And nine months later, beneath the tender gaze of the moon, we shared a kiss that sealed our fate.

  • On Flowers and Signs

    Abrar: Join us as we reminisce about a Valentine's Day filled with unexpected twists. You see, no one had ever bought me flowers before, and I had quietly whispered my true desire to Aljosa – "Tulips, that's what I've always yearned for."
    Aljosa: With plans to present her with a bouquet of roses, I set out on a mission to make the day unforgettable.
    Abrar: But as fate would have it, when Aljosa arrived at the flower shop, all the roses had been claimed by other romantics. What remained was a vibrant bouquet of tulips, my secret wish.
    Aljosa: Imagine her astonishment when I handed her those tulips! Her eyes sparkled with wonder, and it was the moment that gave me the courage to ask her the question which would forever tie us together.

  • It Was Like Destiny

    Abrar: Journey back in time with us to a pivotal moment in Aljosa's life. At the age of nine, he played the role of Sheikh Ahri Sahari in a primary school play.
    Aljosa: Little did we know that this childhood role held a foreshadowing of our future. In the play, Sheikh Ahri Sahari left Europe to seek destiny in the desert, where he met his beloved between Mecca and Medina.
    Abrar: Fast forward twenty years, and Aljosa found himself in Bahrain, in the very heart of the desert. It was there, beneath the expansive desert sky, that our paths crossed.
    Aljosa: Our souls recognized each other, and the echoes of our hearts' whispers revealed our destiny, just as foretold by Sheikh Ahri Sahari in that childhood play.
